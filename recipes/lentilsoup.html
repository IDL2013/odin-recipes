Granny's Lentil Soup

Image obtained from 'Scottish Scran'


Description

I grew up on this, as did my Mum. A not-so-secret recipe passed through the generations!

Ingredients

Serves: 4

  • Olive oil (Glug)
  • Onion, diced (1 large)
  • Garlic, finely chopped (Two cloves)
  • Carrot, peeled and finely chopped (One)
  • Celery, peeled and finely chopped (One)
  • Tomato puree (1 tbsp)
  • Thyme leaves (sprig)
  • Red lentils, dried (200g)
  • Vegetable stock (400ml)

Steps

  1. Add olive oil to a hot pan and saute onions, celery and carrot under a medium heat until opaque and slightly golden (5-7 mins)
  2. Meanwhile, rinse lentils in cold tap water until water runs clear, then drain
  3. Add the garlic, tomato puree and a sprig of thyme, and cook for a further 1-2 mins
  4. Stir in the lentils, followed by 400ml of vegetable stock
  5. Simmer for 30 minutes until the lentils have cooked down
  6. Season to taste and serve hot with crusty bread.
