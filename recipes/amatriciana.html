Penne Amatriciana

Image obtained from 'The Spruce Eats'


Description

A staple from my student years, this simple, comforting dish can be made in less than 15 minutes.

Ingredients

Serves: 2

  • Olive oil (Glug)
  • Onion, diced (1 large)
  • Garlic, finely chopped (Two cloves)
  • Red chilli, deseeded and finely chopped (One)
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved (300g)
  • Bacon lardons, smoked (100g)
  • Basil leaves, torn (Handful)
  • Penne pasta (180g)
  • Optional: parmesan, grated (to taste)

Steps

  1. Add olive oil to a hot pan and fry bacon for 5 minutes. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  2. Heat onions in the same pan under a medium heat until opaque and slightly golden (5-7 mins)
  3. Meanwhile, bring a pote of water to boil and cook pasta for 10 - 12 minutes
  4. Add the garlic and chillies to the onions and saute for further 1-2 mins
  5. Add the bacon and cherry tomatoes back to the pan with a tablespoon of the pasta water
  6. Continue on medium heat until the tomatoes have cooked down (5 minutes)
  7. Drain the pasta and mix into the pan with the sauce
  8. Tear in fresh basil and season to taste
  9. Serve immediately, with grated parmesan and a drizzle of good olive oil.

Buon appetito!
