Aubergine Parmigiana

Image obtained from 'BBC Good Food'


Description

Such a tasty dish. It's one of those amazing meals that tastes even better the second day!

Ingredients

Serves: 4

  • Olive oil (Glug)
  • Aubergines, sliced lengthways at thickness of a Â£2 coin (3)
  • Onion, diced (1 large)
  • Garlic, finely chopped (Two cloves)
  • Carrot, peeled and finely chopped (One)
  • Celery, peeled and finely chopped (One)
  • Tomato puree (1 tbsp)
  • Oregano (1 tbsp)
  • Chopped tomatoes (2 cans)
  • Pinch of sugar
  • Red wine vinegar (1 tbsp)
  • Mozzarella cheese (100g)
  • Parmesan cheese (100g)

Steps

  1. Add olive oil to a hot pan and fry the aubergine slices in batches. Season and set aside.
  2. Add another glug of oil to the pan and fry the onions, carrot and celery for 10 minutes over a medium heat.
  3. Once the onions and celery are translucent, add the garlic, tomato puree and oregano, and cook for a further 1-2 mins
  4. Deglaze the pan with the red wine vinegar before adding the sugar
  5. Add the tomatoes and simmer for 30 minutes until the all the ingredients have merged together
  6. Preheat oven to 180C (fan).
  7. Add a layer of tomato sauce to a large ovenproof dish. Then create a 'lasagne layer' of aubergine slices. Add chunks of mozzarella and parmesan
  8. Continue to layer until all aubergines and sauce have been used.
  9. Sprinkle with a final generous layer of parmesan and mozzarella, some basil leaves and a glug of olive oil
  10. Cook on middle shelf of oven for 30 minutes, until golden brown and bubbling.
  11. Season to taste and serve hot with crusty bread, a nice salad and a glass of red wine.
